Our Story

Kenya is Our
Home & Passion

We don't just show you Kenya — we share it the way we know it best: intimately, honestly, unforgettably.

Founded in Nairobi in 2023, Cotesa Tours was built on a single belief: the best safari is led by people who were born in this landscape, who understand its rhythms, speak its languages, and feel it in their bones.

From the Masai Mara to the Aberdare forests, from Diani Beach to Lake Nakuru — every itinerary carries the knowledge of our guides and the warmth of our people.

Born in the Bush

Our guides grew up in and around Kenya's national parks. Their knowledge isn't from a textbook — it's lived, inherited, and deeply personal.

🌿

Eco-Responsible

We partner only with lodges and camps that practise low-impact tourism and actively support wildlife conservation and local communities.

Safe & Supported
