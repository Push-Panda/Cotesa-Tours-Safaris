Our Story

Kenya is Our
Home & Passion

We don't just show you Kenya — we share it with you the way we know it best: intimately, honestly, and unforgettably.

Founded in Nairobi in 2010, Cotesa Tours was built on a single belief: that the best safari is one led by people who were born in this landscape, who understand its rhythms, speak its languages, and feel it in their bones.

From the sweeping plains of the Masai Mara to the forests of Aberdare, from Diani Beach to Lake Nakuru, every itinerary we craft carries the knowledge of our guides and the warmth of our people.
